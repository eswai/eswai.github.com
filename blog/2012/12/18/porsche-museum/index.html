Porsche Museum With RX100

Dec 18th, 2012

SLT-A57 is nice camera, especially with DT 35mm F1.8 lens. However it is difficult to take with me every time. Actually I have Canon Power shot S90 which was not so bad before, but apparently outputs out of S90 is far from A57, rather close to iPhone 4S.

So I got a new compact camera Cyber-shot DSC-RX100, which has a very good reputaion so far.

  • Very fine photo, really much closer to A57.
  • Quite similer operation to SLT-A57, which I did’t expect.
  • DxO Optics Pro is able to handle RAW from RX100.
  • My first Carl Zeiss :)

I went to Porsche Museum in Stuttgart to try my new camera.

What a elegant tail!

The next photo shows some outlet or inlet of Boxter concept. Every detail of the car is so sophisticated.
