Strasbourg

Dec 2nd, 2012

Yesterday I went to Strasbourg in France, very close to Germany. I visited the cathedral in the center of the city. It was so amazing building - a large number of precise sculpture ouside, beautiful stained glasses in the hall.

There were Christmans market around the cathedral. I did’t spend not so long there, but it was interesting to see many colorfull things.

Every restaurant in the center was full even after 14 o’clock, I moved to the restaurant between the main station and the cathedral. I had stew of 3 kinds of meat which was simple and a bit salty. But I enjoyed it because such stew, which may take a bit long time to cook, can not be seen in Germany.

It was so cold yesterday.
